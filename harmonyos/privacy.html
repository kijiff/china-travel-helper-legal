Privacy Policy — HarmonyOS

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the HarmonyOS version of China Travel Helper ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, the App may collect the following types of data:

  • Voice Data: When using speech recognition, your voice is sent to Alibaba Cloud DashScope for transcription. Voice data is processed in transit and not permanently stored by us.
  • Camera & Image Data: When using menu translation or photo-based features, images are sent to Alibaba Cloud DashScope for visual analysis. Images are transmitted temporarily and not retained.
  • Text Input: Translation text, chat messages, and search queries are sent to Alibaba Cloud DashScope for processing.
  • Location Data: When using map and navigation features, your approximate or precise location is used to provide navigation services. Location data is only accessed with your explicit permission and is not stored by us.
  • Device Information: Basic device metadata (OS version, device model) may be used for compatibility and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used solely for:

  • Providing translation, speech recognition, and text-to-speech services (via Alibaba Cloud DashScope)
  • Providing map navigation (via Google Maps, Amap, Baidu Maps, or Apple Maps)
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Processing in-app purchases (via Huawei AGC)

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Alibaba Cloud DashScope — Provides translation, speech recognition, text-to-speech, and image analysis. Data is transmitted to dashscope.aliyuncs.com for real-time processing only. Voice, text, and image inputs are not used for AI model training and are not retained after processing. Privacy Policy
  • Huawei AppGallery Connect (AGC) — Processes in-app purchases. Privacy Policy
  • Map Services — Google Maps, Amap (高德), Baidu Maps, and Apple Maps for navigation. No personal data is shared beyond what is necessary for routing.

4. Data Storage & Security

App content (survival guide, price data, emergency contacts) is cached locally on your device. No personal data is stored on our server.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.

5. Permissions We Request

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Camera — For menu translation and photo-based features
  • Microphone — For speech recognition
  • Location — For map navigation
  • Internet — For all network-based services

You may revoke any permission at any time via your device Settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Revoke permissions at any time in device Settings
  • Clear app data to remove all locally stored content
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.

隐私政策 — HarmonyOS

最后更新：2026年7月18日

本隐私政策适用于 China Travel Helper 的 HarmonyOS 版本（"本应用"）。

1. 我们收集的信息

为提供服务，本应用可能收集以下类型的数据：

  • 语音数据：使用语音识别时，您的语音将发送至阿里云百炼（DashScope）进行转写。语音数据仅在传输过程中临时处理，我们不会永久存储。
  • 相机与图像数据：使用菜单翻译或拍照功能时，图像将发送至阿里云百炼进行视觉分析。图像仅临时传输，不会被保留。
  • 文本输入：翻译文本、聊天消息和搜索查询将发送至阿里云百炼进行处理。
  • 位置数据：使用地图和导航功能时，您的粗略或精确位置将用于提供导航服务。位置数据仅在您明确授权后使用，我们不会存储。
  • 设备信息：基本的设备元数据（操作系统版本、设备型号）可能用于兼容性分析和数据统计。

2. 数据使用方式

所有收集的数据仅用于：

  • 提供翻译、语音识别和语音合成服务（通过阿里云百炼）
  • 提供地图导航（通过 Google Maps、高德地图、百度地图或 Apple Maps）
  • 改善应用性能和用户体验
  • 处理应用内购买（通过华为 AGC）

3. 第三方服务

本应用集成了以下第三方服务：

  • 阿里云百炼（DashScope） — 提供翻译、语音识别、语音合成和图像分析服务。数据发送至 dashscope.aliyuncs.com 仅用于实时处理。语音、文本和图像输入不会用于 AI 模型训练，处理后不会保留。隐私政策
  • 华为 AppGallery Connect（AGC） — 处理应用内购买。隐私政策
  • 地图服务 — Google Maps、高德地图、百度地图和 Apple Maps 用于导航，不共享个人数据。

4. 数据存储与安全

应用内容（生存指南、价格数据、紧急联系人）缓存在您的设备本地。我们不在服务器上存储任何个人数据。

我们采用行业标准的安全措施保护数据传输。所有 API 通信使用 HTTPS 加密。

  • 网络 — 用于所有网络服务
