Privacy Policy — Android

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Android version of China Travel Helper ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, the App may collect the following types of data:

  • Voice Data: When using speech recognition, your voice is sent to Alibaba Cloud DashScope for transcription. Voice data is processed in transit and not permanently stored by us.
  • Camera & Image Data: When using menu translation or photo-based features, images are sent to Alibaba Cloud DashScope for visual analysis. Images are transmitted temporarily and not retained.
  • Text Input: Translation text, chat messages, and search queries are sent to Alibaba Cloud DashScope for processing.
  • Location Data: When using map and navigation features, your approximate or precise location is used to provide navigation services. Location data is only accessed with your explicit permission and is not stored by us.
  • Usage & Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage statistics (e.g., feature usage frequency, crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics) to improve the App.
  • Device Information: Basic device metadata (OS version, device model) may be used for compatibility and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used solely for:

  • Providing translation and voice services (via Alibaba Cloud DashScope)
  • Providing map navigation (via Google Maps, Amap, Baidu Maps, or Apple Maps)
  • Improving app performance and fixing crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Understanding feature usage to improve user experience (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Processing in-app purchases (via Google Play Billing)

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Alibaba Cloud DashScope — Provides translation, speech recognition, text-to-speech, and image analysis. Data is transmitted to dashscope.aliyuncs.com. Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase — Provides analytics (Firebase Analytics) and crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics). Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — Processes in-app purchases. Privacy Policy
  • ExchangeRate-API — Provides real-time currency exchange rates. No personal data is sent.

4. Data Storage & Security

App content (survival guide, price data, emergency contacts) is cached locally on your device. Remote content updates are fetched from our server (47.239.160.6) via encrypted or plain HTTP. No personal data is stored on our server.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.

5. Permissions We Request

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • CAMERA — For menu translation and photo-based features
  • RECORD_AUDIO — For speech recognition
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — For map navigation
  • INTERNET — For all network-based services

You may revoke any permission at any time via your device Settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Revoke permissions at any time in device Settings
  • Clear app data to remove all locally stored content
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.

隐私政策 — Android

最后更新：2026年6月23日

本隐私政策适用于 China Travel Helper 的 Android 版本（"本应用"）。

1. 我们收集的信息

为提供服务，本应用可能收集以下类型的数据：

  • 语音数据：使用语音识别时，您的语音将发送至阿里云百炼（DashScope）进行转写。语音数据仅在传输过程中临时处理，我们不会永久存储。
  • 相机与图像数据：使用菜单翻译或拍照功能时，图像将发送至阿里云百炼进行视觉分析。图像仅临时传输，不会被保留。
  • 文本输入：翻译文本、聊天消息和搜索查询将发送至阿里云百炼进行处理。
  • 位置数据：使用地图和导航功能时，您的粗略或精确位置将用于提供导航服务。位置数据仅在您明确授权后使用，我们不会存储。
  • 使用与分析数据：Firebase Analytics 收集匿名使用统计数据（如功能使用频率），Firebase Crashlytics 收集崩溃报告，用于改善应用体验。
  • 设备信息：基本的设备元数据（操作系统版本、设备型号）可能用于兼容性分析和数据统计。

2. 数据使用方式

所有收集的数据仅用于：

  • 提供翻译和语音服务（通过阿里云百炼）
  • 提供地图导航（通过 Google Maps、高德地图、百度地图或 Apple Maps）
  • 改善应用性能和修复崩溃（通过 Firebase Crashlytics）
  • 了解功能使用情况以优化用户体验（通过 Firebase Analytics）
  • 处理应用内购买（通过 Google Play Billing）

3. 第三方服务

本应用集成了以下第三方服务，请参阅其各自的隐私政策：

  • 阿里云百炼（DashScope） — 提供翻译、语音识别、语音合成和图像分析服务。数据发送至 dashscope.aliyuncs.com。隐私政策
  • Google Firebase — 提供数据分析（Firebase Analytics）和崩溃报告（Firebase Crashlytics）。隐私政策
  • Google Play Billing — 处理应用内购买。隐私政策
  • ExchangeRate-API — 提供实时汇率数据，不发送个人数据。

4. 数据存储与安全

应用内容（生存指南、价格数据、紧急联系人）缓存在您的设备本地。远程内容更新从我们的服务器（47.239.160.6）获取。我们不在服务器上存储任何个人数据。

我们采用行业标准的安全措施保护数据传输。所有 API 通信使用 HTTPS 加密。

5. 申请的权限

本应用申请以下 Android 权限：

  • CAMERA（相机） — 用于菜单翻译和拍照功能
  • RECORD_AUDIO（录音） — 用于语音识别
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION（位置） — 用于地图导航
  • INTERNET（网络） — 用于所有网络服务

您可以随时在设备设置中撤销任何权限。

6. 您的权利

  • 随时在设备设置中撤销权限
  • 清除应用数据以删除所有本地存储内容
  • 就隐私相关问题联系我们

7. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童数据。

8. 政策更新

我们可能会更新本政策。继续使用即表示同意更新后的内容。
